How It Works

Three steps to a validated design.

01
Discovery Consultation

We dive deep into your drone design. Where are you in the build process? What keeps you up at night about the design? What does this product need to do well? That conversation shapes everything that comes after.

02
CAD and Simulation Analysis

I work through your CAD files in two passes. First, structural and mechanical integrity — loads, joints, material decisions, and how the frame holds together under stress. Then flight and system dynamics — how the design actually behaves in the air, where performance breaks down, and what the simulation says about real operating conditions.

03
Validation Report and Recommendations

You receive a clear, prioritized report: what will work, what needs refinement, and exactly what to change — so your team builds right the first time.
